Privacy Policy - Smart Todo List & Calendar Chrome Extension

1. Data Collection and Storage
- This extension does not collect or transmit any personal data.
- All information (tasks, settings, and preferences) is stored locally in your browser.
- No data is sent to external servers or shared with third parties.

2. Browser Permissions
- The extension only requires access to the "New Tab" page to function.
- It does not request access to browsing history, cookies, or other sensitive data.

3. Security
- Your data remains exclusively on your device.
- You maintain complete control over your data.
- You can remove all data simply by uninstalling the extension.

4. Updates
- This privacy policy may be updated as new features are added.
- Significant changes will be communicated through the extension's update notes.

5. Contact
- For privacy-related inquiries, you can open an issue in the project repository.
